Introduction

Purpose

AnimeKomo is devoted to provide ad-free anime listings for anime watchers. We list genres that you might be interested in, and provide advice. Every anime we list will have a brief introduction and where to watch it.

You will never run out of anime to watch is our goal. For new anime watcher, we can give you advice on what anime you should watch to start becoming an anime fan!

Profit

Unlike other "free" websites, they always get something out of you. It could be your data, ads they display on their websites, or annoying pop-ups that gives you no user experience. However, that makes us different.

AnimeKomo is devoted to provide you an ad-free environment, and our website will never use any technique to track your online footage. Things that keep us running is your support and sponsorships. We have strict rules when selecting sponsors and displaying them.

We get thousands of requests from anime companies to rank them higher on the list, but we decline them all. We list anime by popularity and rating by users, and sponsorships we take is to write reviews for their anime.

Not all anime listed on AnimeKomo has an review, and that is when we take sponsorships and review them.
